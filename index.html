We see the same strangers everyday. Let's start talking.

FishBowl is a revolutionary social discovery app aimed at solving the "familiar stranger" problem. With FishBowl running in the background, your device will keep a log of everyone you interact with over the course of a day, allowing you to go back and find out more about the people around you.

24 Hour Timeline

See a comprehensive timeline of all the people who you've interacted with over the last 24 hours.

Social Media Integration

You can connect several social media accounts to FishBowl in order to help connect with people who you've met.

Bluetooth Low Energy

Our application uses low energy Bluetooth to reduce strain on the battery.

FishBowl is for everyone.

Up and running in two taps.

Simply install the app and forget. FishBowl will silently scan for other nearby devices in a special low-energy mode. When another user is close enough, your phones will trade information like name and Facebook profile. You can browse your timeline at any point to review your interactions.
